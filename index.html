About


My current aspiration in the tech world is to design and implement robust, scalable software solutions to solve the world's complex problems. With 4 years of experience in software development, I've had the opportunity to work with diverse teams and industries. Through all of this I have strengthened my skills in understanding complex business problems and delivering efficient, easily modifiable solutions.

My true strength lies in critical thinking. Whether it's debugging intricate issues or architecting new systems, I leverage my analytical mindset to dissect problems, identify root causes, and provide an array of solutions.

Wait why discover many solutions? Doesn't that introduce more complexity? The most creative part of our engineering occupations is that we have the ability to achieve the same outcome in so many ways. Add in the fact that no problem is ever comprised of a single decision, and these decisions are never dichotomous, and the tangled web of all the possible paths to the same outcome exposes the beauty of engineering.

In a more serious sense, an understanding of many solutions exposes the best one. By having all the solutions at hand we can compare them on well-defined metrics and truly find the perfect fit. My varied experience allows me to understand these solutions in web development, mobile app development, business analytics, product, and infrastructure related contexts. The ability to communicate and share my understanding to others always places me in a critical position on any team, in any industry and that is the strong technical tool set I bring to the table.
